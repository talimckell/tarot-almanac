## 1. What the Service is

The Tarot Almanac calculates tarot cards and readings from dates using a fixed, deterministic formula (mod-22 arithmetic on the Major Arcana, with a documented two-stage process for Minor Arcana day cards). Every result is derived from math you can verify yourself; nothing is shuffled or randomly drawn. On top of these deterministic results, we offer optional AI-synthesized interpretive readings, which are clearly labeled as such and are a separate, generated layer distinct from the underlying chart math.

**The Service is offered for entertainment, self-reflection, and educational purposes only.** It is not a substitute for professional medical, mental health, legal, financial, or other professional advice. Don't use it as the basis for major life, medical, financial, or legal decisions. If you're in crisis or need professional support, please seek out a qualified professional.
