The twenty-two Major Arcana run as one arc, the Fool's Journey, and it moves through three
stages: Initiation, Testing, and Reckoning.

## Initiation: cards 0–7

<!-- The opening movement: forming a self. Your read of the band. -->

The Fool (0), the Magician (1), the High Priestess (2), the Empress (3), the Emperor (4),
the Hierophant (5), the Lovers (6), and the Chariot (7).

## Testing: cards 8–14

<!-- The middle movement: the self meets what tries it. Your read. -->

Strength (8), the Hermit (9), the Wheel of Fortune (10), Justice (11), the Hanged One (12),
Death (13), and Temperance (14).

## Reckoning: cards 15–21

<!-- The closing movement: what the journey resolves into. Your read. -->

The Devil (15), the Tower (16), the Star (17), the Moon (18), the Sun (19), Judgement (20),
and the World (21).
